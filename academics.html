Beliefs on Leadership and teamwork

Leadership to me means one person (or many people) aiding others in an assertive way to achieve one common goal. And with teamwork, I believe it means a group of people working together as one to, like I stated in my leadership definition, achieve one goal. To me, the two go hand and hand with each other.
